About sissy

Get notified when your grades for Hochschule Bonn-Rhein-Sieg have updated. sissy is completely open source, check it out on GitHub at https://github.com/MuscleRumble/sissy.

Privacy Policy

sissy does not collect any personal information. Your data is securely stored in iOS Keychain Services.
